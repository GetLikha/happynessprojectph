HappY Kids Project

The HappYness Kids Project aims to make a difference to children living in severe poverty that have experience deprivation of needs that halt their growth and well being.

Our goal is to make is to make Filipino children happY through kindness, love and holistic support.

Our goal is to make is to make Filipino children happY through kindness, love and holistic support.

We started with 20 kids and we served 150 kids as of December 2022.

HappY Nanays

The HappY Nanays Livelihood program aims to provide livelihood and sustainable related training for the mothers of HappY Kids aimed to augment their income.

As we help them create continuous income stream the living condition of our HappY kids will also improve.

Rag-Making Workshop

The HappYness Rag-Making Project shares a goal that includes: learn and develop skills, provide opportunity for micro business, women empowerment and help vulnerable families attain financial stability.

Happy Hearts
Partnerships

Our HappY Hearts Partnerships program connects individuals and corporations to our vetted partner institutions of orphanages, elderly homes, shelters, communities, and schools.

We will help you plan, organize and execute your funded outreach activities at no extra cost.
